PUBG

PUBG (Player Unknown Battlegrounds) is similar to other hunger games.PUBG was released in 2017 and became popular in South Asian countries.

Basics of gameplay

Basics of gameplay is based on all against all.The starting is all like other hunger games in which you have to collect weapons and items to stay alive.The ground is an 8x8 km island with 100 players ending with the last player surviving.The game modes can be solo, duo and squads depending on choice.
The game starts with jumping from a plane and parachuting onto an island. After landing,your mission is to collect weapons and medical supplies.The whole playground is playable at the start but starts decreasing with time.This forces players to condensed down within the playzone. Each game can take 30 minutes if you are the winner. You can survive upto last or can die as soon as you land on the ground.
You can learn more about game by visiting PUBJ

Weapons

The weapons used are of different types. Most used are

  • Short guns
  • Long range guns
  • Sniper guns
  • Pistols

Medical items

Medical items are of different types to boost energy and healing wounds.

  • Medical kit
  • Energy drinks
  • Adrenaline shot
  • First aid kit
  • Painkillers

Strategies

Fighting and looting

You need to keep looting limited to participate and gain experience in the game. Fighting and dominating others can help more in gathering good weapons and other stuff.

Communication

Communication is very essential during attacking and defending. Good communication with team members can help in making plans and winning battles. Camping can help you in long term survival.Camping is effective way to defend yourself.
