Password Management

Password management is the technique a person must follow in order to stay secure. These techniques require an individual to create passwords that are difficult for the other to guess. Although simple passwords are easy to memorize they put a person in a vulnerable position where they are more likely to get hacked. Using the same password everywhere has become a common practice. This is also something that can make it easier for someone to get hacked.

Rules to create Passwords

When creating accounts on websites, you are typically given a set of rules to follow when creating a password. These rules help make sure your passwords are difficult, making your account more secure. Using uppercase letters and numbers is common. However, sometimes these rules are difficult to follow since you might be required to use various symbols and have a certain number of characters. But these are the things that will ensure safety and privacy.

Commonly Used Passwords

Passwords play the role of keeping a person’s information confidential. This information can be something minor like photos on social media, or something major like banking. This is why websites or institutions have a set of rules a person must follow when creating their passwords. These rules help make passwords difficult for others to guess. Commonly used passwords include a person’s name, last name, age, birthday as well as simple numbers like “0000” or “1234.” These passwords are common because they are easy to remember. Four percent of users use the top 100 passwords that are common.

Securing Your Passwords

A good way to remember your passwords and keep them secure is by writing them down. Written passwords are easily approachable and kept private so that no one else can see them. One more approachable way to keep passwords safe is using a password manager. Password manager keeps your passwords secure and you don’t need to secure them on the browser. Password manager creates strong passwords that are difficult to get hacked and you don’t even need to memorize them.
